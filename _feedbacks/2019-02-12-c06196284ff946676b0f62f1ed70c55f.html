Hello,
I have developed an application for bloggers and readers for iPhone and iPad.

The key points are:
- Easily find blogs with to a dedicated search engine (While the web search engines display only the same already popular blogs)
- Discover blogs by topics, by trends
- The reader can follow a blog in one top (don't need to create a bookmark in the web browser)
- A news feed to get all new posts in one page
- Because the reader has all its blogs in one place, he has more time to read posts and discover blogs.
- Importing an existing WordPress only takes a few minutes. And keeping it updated with the new posts from your WordPress site needs only one tap.

The application has been launched on the Apple app store and I’m looking for bloggers that would import their WordPress blog in the app. Or, at least, bloggers who would give me the permission to import their blog in the app.

You can preview and download the app on: https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/postboard-blogs-and-forums/id1439053064?ls=1&mt=8

You can have more info on https://www.postboard.app

Best regards,
Fabrice Noui
